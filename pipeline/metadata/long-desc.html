Deliberate Self-Harm (DSH) annotator

This annotator marks up mentions of deliberate self-harm (DSH) in clinical texts. The algorithm determines whether a mention is negated or not, whether it is current or historical and whether it is relevant or not, as defined by the parameters of the study. The type of self-harm is also annotated in a normalised form.
